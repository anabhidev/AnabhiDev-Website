FAQ

Common questions before booking

Everything you need to know about booking a Bali tour with Anabhi Journey.

Can I pay in USD, AUD, or EUR?
+
Prices are displayed in multiple currencies for your reference, but all payments are accepted in Indonesian Rupiah (IDR) only. Converted prices are indicative estimates.
Can I request a fully custom itinerary?
+
Yes! We specialize in custom private itineraries. Tell us your destinations, travel pace, group size, and budget — and we'll craft a day-by-day plan. Initial consultation is free with no commitment.
Is car rental available without a driver?
+
Our vehicle rental is with a driver only. This ensures you travel safely with local navigation expertise, especially on Bali's mountain and coastal roads.
How do I confirm and pay for a booking?
+
After selecting your package and sending an inquiry via WhatsApp, we'll confirm availability and send IDR payment details. A down payment (DP) is required to secure your booking, with the balance payable on the day.
What is included in a private tour package?
+
Private tours include a dedicated guide, private vehicle, fuel, hotel pickup, and entrance fees (where stated). Meals and personal expenses are typically not included unless specified in the package details.

Who We Are

Anabhi Journey

Founded in Seminyak, Bali, Anabhi Journey was born from the belief that travel should be deeply personal — not a rushed checklist of tourist spots, but a genuine connection with the island's soul.

Our founders are Balinese locals who grew up surrounded by the island's culture, temples, and traditions. We've been guiding international travelers since 2019, and every trip we create is shaped by that lived-in knowledge.

Today, we serve solo travelers, couples, honeymooners, and families from across the world — crafting private itineraries, open trips, and custom experiences that stay with people long after they return home.
