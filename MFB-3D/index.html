Our Story

Born from the Sea,
Raised in Bali

Tucked between the warm breezes of the Indian Ocean and the verdant hills of Bali, Miss Fish is a celebration of the sea in its most refined form. We believe every great dish begins before the kitchen — at the tide, at the reef, at the hands of fishermen who rise before dawn.

Our menu honours the island's extraordinary marine abundance, elevated with precision, restraint, and a quiet obsession for flavour. Here, the ocean is not just an ingredient — it is the entire story.
