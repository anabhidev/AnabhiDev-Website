Proven Results

From Problem to Solution

A closer look at two apps — the real problem behind them, how they were solved, and what changed.

Automation

SpinGather

PROBLEM

Generic tools like Wheel of Names meant HR had to manually track and record every winner by hand — slow and error-prone for large staff gatherings.

SOLUTION

A custom spin-wheel app that auto-confirms each winner, instantly logs them to Google Sheets, and sends a Telegram notification — zero manual tracking.

RESULT

Used for a 110-person staff gathering (one spin per employee, so everyone won a prize) — HR walked away with a complete winner list and zero post-event reconciliation.

Education

Learning Management Systems

PROBLEM

Off-the-shelf LMS platforms were either a poor fit for the company's training needs or came with steep monthly subscription costs.

SOLUTION

A custom LMS built from scratch — including offline training attendance forms — tailored exactly to how the company trains its staff.

RESULT

Reduced reliance on in-person training sessions, letting staff complete modules at their own pace with automatically issued certificates.
